Subhash Chandra has gained knowledge and experience since his childhood from his grandfather Late Jagan Nath Ji Goenka and Late S.N. Goenka (Vipassanacharya) and the gurus/ teachers inside the thousands of people, out of lacs of people he met throughout his life.

He has seen all kinds of Ups & Downs, Success & Failures, sincere individuals as well as not so sincere people who claimed friendship.

He has gained knowledge/wisdom by observing people who were seen a happy, also who were suffering from poverty, sickness, being lonely, under stress due to debt burden, bad relations with their spouse, family member (s), property disputes, other kind of litigations, weakness of various kind, failure in schools/colleges and facing other kind of failures in life, Joblessness, losses in business, sadness and depression.
