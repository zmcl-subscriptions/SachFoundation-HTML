Foundation

Subhash Chandra Foundation, the philanthropic arm of Essel Group is established to share, contribute and collaborate for a prosperous society – a way to build inclusive & elevated communities. The objective of the foundation is to help create and support models across sectors spanning from the mainstream to the niche sectors that provide for an equitable and sustainable society....
